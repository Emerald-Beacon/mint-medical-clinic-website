The Science

Three Peptides, One Powerful Formula

Each peptide targets a different aspect of skin rejuvenation for comprehensive anti-aging results.

GHK-Cu (Copper Peptide)

The Collagen Builder

  • Stimulates collagen, elastin, and glycosaminoglycan synthesis
  • Tightens skin and improves firmness
  • Promotes tissue remodeling
  • Attracts immune cells to damaged areas
  • Supports function of dermal fibroblasts

GHK naturally declines by 50%+ after age 60, contributing to visible aging.

BPC-157

The Healer

  • Creates new blood vessel networks (angiogenesis)
  • Delivers nutrients to skin cells
  • Accelerates wound and scar healing
  • Provides the "delivery system" for collagen synthesis
  • Reduces inflammation at the cellular level

Without blood flow, collagen has raw materials but no delivery system.

TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4)

The Regenerator

  • Promotes tissue regeneration
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Supports cell migration and repair
  • Enhances flexibility of skin tissue
  • Complements GHK-Cu and BPC-157 actions

TB-500 adds the regenerative power for complete skin restoration.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Klow Glow Peptide Cocktail?

Klow Glow is a synergistic peptide blend combining GHK-Cu (copper peptide), BPC-157, and TB-500. This cocktail targets skin rejuvenation from multiple angles—stimulating collagen production, accelerating healing, and reducing inflammation for a youthful, radiant complexion.

How does Klow Glow improve skin?

GHK-Cu stimulates collagen and elastin production for firmer skin. BPC-157 creates new blood vessels to deliver nutrients to skin cells. TB-500 reduces inflammation and promotes tissue regeneration. Together, they work synergistically to reverse signs of aging and restore a healthy glow.

How is Klow Glow administered?

Klow Glow can be administered via subcutaneous injection or topically, depending on your treatment goals. Your physician will recommend the best method based on your skin concerns and desired results. Injectable forms provide systemic benefits while topicals target specific areas.

How long until I see results?

Many patients notice improved skin hydration and subtle glow within 2-4 weeks. Visible reduction in fine lines typically occurs at 4-8 weeks. Maximum results including improved firmness and texture are usually seen at 12+ weeks of consistent use.

What are the side effects?

The peptides in Klow Glow are generally well-tolerated. Rare side effects may include mild irritation at injection sites. Some users have reported temporary skin sensitivity with copper peptides, though this is uncommon. Your physician will monitor your progress.

Can Klow Glow replace my skincare routine?

Klow Glow works from the inside out by stimulating your body's own collagen and healing processes. It complements topical skincare but doesn't replace sunscreen, cleansing, and moisturizing. For best results, combine with a good skincare routine and sun protection.
