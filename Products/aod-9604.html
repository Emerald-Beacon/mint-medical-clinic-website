Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AOD-9604?

AOD-9604 is a synthetic 16-amino acid peptide fragment derived from human growth hormone (amino acids 176-191). It was designed to capture growth hormone's fat-burning properties without the broader metabolic effects.

How does AOD-9604 work?

AOD-9604 stimulates lipolysis (fat breakdown) and inhibits lipogenesis (fat formation) by interacting with beta-3 adrenergic receptors in fat cells. It increases the expression of these fat-burning receptors without affecting blood sugar or IGF-1 levels.

Does AOD-9604 affect blood sugar?

No. Unlike full growth hormone, AOD-9604 does not appear to have adverse effects on insulin sensitivity, making it a potentially safer option for those concerned about metabolic effects.

What are the side effects?

AOD-9604 has an excellent safety profile based on clinical trials with over 900 participants. It was found to be generally safe with minimal adverse effects and no genotoxic activities.

How long until I see results?

Results vary based on individual factors and lifestyle. AOD-9604 works best when combined with proper nutrition and exercise. Your physician will help set realistic expectations during your consultation.

Is AOD-9604 FDA approved?

AOD-9604 is not currently FDA approved but is available through compounding pharmacies under physician supervision. It has extensive safety data from clinical trials.
