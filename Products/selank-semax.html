Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Selank and Semax?

Semax is primarily a cognitive enhancer that boosts focus, memory, and mental clarity by increasing BDNF levels. Selank is primarily an anxiolytic (anti-anxiety) peptide that promotes calm and mood stability by modulating GABA receptors. Think of Semax as the "gas pedal" for cognitive performance, while Selank is the "traction control" for emotional stability.

Can Selank and Semax be used together?

Yes, Selank and Semax are often used together as they complement each other well. Semax enhances cognitive function and focus while Selank reduces anxiety and stress, allowing for optimal mental performance without overstimulation.

How are these peptides administered?

Both Selank and Semax are typically administered as intranasal sprays for rapid absorption directly to the brain. This bypasses the digestive system for faster onset of effects. Your physician will provide specific dosing instructions.

How quickly do they work?

Effects can be felt within 15-30 minutes of nasal administration. Cognitive benefits may accumulate over days to weeks of regular use. Many users report noticeable improvements in focus and mood within the first few days.

Are there side effects?

Both peptides are generally well-tolerated. Unlike benzodiazepines, Selank does not cause sedation, amnesia, withdrawal, or dependence. Some users may experience mild nasal irritation. Side effects are rare and typically minimal.

Are Selank and Semax FDA approved?

These peptides are not FDA-approved in the United States. They are registered medicines in Russia with decades of clinical use there. Our physicians prescribe them based on the available research and clinical experience, with appropriate medical oversight.
