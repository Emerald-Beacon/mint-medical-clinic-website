Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BPC-157?

BPC-157 is a synthetic peptide consisting of 15 amino acids, derived from a body protection compound (BPC) found naturally in the stomach. It's known for its regenerative properties in wound healing, muscle recovery, and digestive health.

What conditions can BPC-157 help with?

BPC-157 promotes repair of muscles, tendons, and ligaments. It's also beneficial for gut health issues like gastric ulcers, leaky gut, IBS, and NSAID-related gut damage. It may also support nerve regeneration.

How does BPC-157 work?

BPC-157 stimulates angiogenesis (new blood vessel growth), boosts collagen production, increases growth hormone receptor expression in tendons, and modulates inflammatory pathways to accelerate healing.

How long until I see results?

Many patients notice improvement within 1-2 weeks, with significant healing occurring over 4-8 weeks depending on the condition being treated. Chronic conditions may require longer treatment periods.

What are the side effects?

BPC-157 is generally well tolerated with no known serious side effects. The primary concern is potential irritation at the injection site, which is common with any injectable medication.

How is BPC-157 administered?

BPC-157 can be administered via subcutaneous injection (under the skin) or intramuscularly near the injury site. Oral forms are also available for gut-related conditions. Your physician will recommend the best method.
